PRIVACY POLICY FOR MOBILE APPLICATIONS

This privacy policy governs your use of Gallant Realm software applications for mobile devices (apps). These apps were all created by Brian Owings, for the enjoyment of his family and friends and for your enjoyment as well. They are meant for use by all ages.

Note that in the following text the words "we" and "us" refer to Gallant Realm, whom at this current time is a sole proprietership owned by Brian Owings.

What information do the apps obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information

The apps do not ask for any personally identifiable information during download or use. Therefore, the only way Gallant Realm would obtain this type of information would be if you were to email us. We will only use this information to resolve whatever issue you report or answer whatever question you have. We will not share the information with other parties and will delete the information as soon as we've completed resolving your issue. We will never contact you except to respond to an issue or question you've reported by email.

Automatically Collected Information

Google Play collects certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile devices unique device ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, the type of mobile Internet browsers you use, and information about the way you use the app. We have no control over this data collection and it does help us to know when the app is misbehaving so that we can fix it.

Beyond Google Play's collection of information, the apps do not collect any other information automatically. All settings and scores in the apps are stored locally, on the device where the apps are installed.

Does the apps collect precise real time location information of the device?

No, the apps do not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the apps?

We do not provide any information obtained by Google Play to any third party. This information is under Google's control however. See Google Play privacy policies regarding sharing of this information.

What are my opt-out rights?

You can stop all collection of information by the apps easily by uninstalling the apps. You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace or network.
